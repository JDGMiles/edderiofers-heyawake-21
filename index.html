Edderiofer’s Heyawake 21

This is a page to experiment with finding a solution to Edderiofer’s “Puzzle #1”.

It is a Heyawake puzzle.

Click a cell to fill it black.
Click it again to fill it white.
Click “Rules” to learn how to play.

When you think you have the solution click “Check” to find out if you are right.

×
Rules

1. Two black cells must not touch side-by-side or top-and-bottom (but diagonally is OK).

2. 
You must be able to reach any white cell from any other white cell.

3. 
The numbers indicate exactly how many black cells there must be in that particular “room”.

4. 
A room which has no number may contain any amount of black cells, or none.

5. 
A horizontal or vertical line of connected white cells must not cross more than two rooms.


Click the “?” in the top left for more information.
